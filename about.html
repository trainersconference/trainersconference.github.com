Internal Trainers Excellence Program

was initiated by Training Management Group in order to implement the world standards in personnel professional development, establish the dynamic process of internal best practices effective sharing and make cooperation with Internal Trainers as much effective as possible.

ITEP includes 4 main directions:

Newsletter

every month you receive the Digest of the most interesting topics related to training and trainers’ activity.


 

Competence Model

model describe the knowledge/skills which have been identified as key factors of trainer’s excellent performance according to world proven visions and our internal experience.

Training Program

includes training and workshops with best external experts and trainers as well as a process of effective sharing of internal experience, approaches and practices.

 

Rewarding Program

basing on clear and simple process of regular rating, the Best Trainer will be defined and rewarded.
